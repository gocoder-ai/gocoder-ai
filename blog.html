Values in the Age of Information

In the age of information, we no longer need worry about finding information but we must focus on finding the right information. Today, there is so much information that sometimes we don't know if it's factual. We should ensure that their the information that they learn form the internet is in fact true. The information should also be valuable, we should not just passively consume "low quality, high quanity" content in social media. The information that we must learn should conribute to our knowledge, skills, and presonalty. We should ensure that the the knowledge we gain from the internet should benefit ourselves and others around us, online and offline.
