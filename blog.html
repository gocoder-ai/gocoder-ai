Values in the Age of Information

In the age of information, we no longer need worry about finding information but we must focus on finding the right information. There is so much information available online, and it can be difficult to know what is true and what is not. We need to be able to identify reliable sources of information, and we need to be able to think critically about the information we find. The information should also be valuable, we should not just passively consume quick content in social media. The information that we must learn should conribute to our knowledge, skills, and personal development. We should ensure that the the knowledge we gain from the internet should benefit ourselves and others around us, online and offline.
